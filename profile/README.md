# I'm Alive 💚

**Someone should know you're okay.**

A simple daily check-in app for people living alone. Tap "I'm OK" once a day — miss it, and your family gets notified automatically.

🌐 [imalive.co](https://imalive.co)
📖 [Why 37 Million Americans Living Alone Need a Daily Check-In](https://imalive.co/blog/why-americans-living-alone-need-daily-check-in)

## How It Works
1. Set your daily check-in time
2. Tap "I'm OK" each day
3. Miss it? Your emergency contacts get notified via push, email, or SMS
